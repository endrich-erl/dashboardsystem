Mission Statement

We ensure the integrity of delivering public services through dedication, hard work, responsible leadership of barangay officials, teamwork, and cooperation among residents of Barangay Pusok. Together, we aim to enhance the quality of life in our community and turn our vision into reality.

Vision Statement

Barangay Pusok envisions a just society that provides Pusokanons with exceptional public services, fostering sustainable economic opportunities, quality education, health and well-being, a clean environment, and a peaceful, drug-free community.
